
The first thing you have to do after installing Bloodhound is import all the `.json` files you got earlier wit bloodhound-python.

Remember you're working as `j.bold` user.

## Steps

1. On Search look for `j.bold` and click on it.
2. Select  `j.bold`  on the map
3. On the side panel click on `Outbound Object Control`.

![[Diagram.svg]]

4. Click on the `Generic All` tag and you'll see the permissions of the user.
